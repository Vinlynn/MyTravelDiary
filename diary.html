Trip to Baguio

February 14, 2014

Destinations:

  • Baguio Botanical Garden is one of the well-known gardens in the Philippines. It is located between Wright Park and Teacher’s Camp along Leonard Wood Road in Baguio City. Baguio Botanical Garden provides tourists with a serene and laid-back environment with tall pine trees, vivid flowers, art galleries (Baguio Arts Guild) and sculptures portraying the different ceremonial of the Cordillera ethnic group can be seen.
  • The Ifugao Rice Terraces epitomize the absolute blending of the physical, socio-cultural, economic, religious, and political environment. Indeed, it is a living cultural landscape of unparalleled beauty. The Ifugao Rice Terraces are the priceless contribution of Philippine ancestors to humanity.
  • A site which popularized the town as the "Strawberry capital in the Philippines". The strawberry farm is the most popular tourist destination in Benguet. Tourists can experience strawberry picking from October to May, farm tour, and do some pasalubong shopping.
